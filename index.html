At Rehoboth healthcare professionals, our mission is to provide compassionate and personalized home health care services to our clients. We strive to improve the quality of life for those we serve, while prioritizing their safety and well-being. We are professionals who truly care.

Our team of skilled and experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering the highest quality of care and support to our clients. We work closely with family and caregivers to ensure that our services meet the unique needs of each individual.

We offer a wide range of home health care services, including skilled nursing, physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, and more. Our goal is to help our valued clients maintain their independence and achieve their health and wellness goals.

By offering person-centered services, we hope to uphold each client's dignity by giving them the necessary tools to make decisions about their lives. We accomplish this by:

- Always treating with respect everyone who is under our care and our employees.
- Encouraging each service user to make their own decisions and exercising their right to do so.
- Upholding and promoting each client's right to freedom.
- Ensuring that every person's right to privacy is upheld at all times and treating all client information with confidentiality.

Services We Offer

Personal Care:
People often require assistance with personal care as they age due to physical or mental deterioration, which makes it harder for them to maintain the degrees of independence they once took for granted. This is where we can be of assistance.

Live-In care:
Live-in care is ideal if you require 24-hour care but don't want to leave your house. Our in-home live-in care services are customised to meet your specific requirements and provide the best possible support while you're at home.
Live-in care enables you to maintain your freedom while remaining in the comfort and familiarity of your own home, regardless of your needs.

Night Care...…..Sleeping Night:
The care assistant stays on the premises for at least eight hours each night and is available for emergencies. This is a good choice if you don't need constant assistance but find it difficult to spend the night alone. With a sleeping night care expert by your side, anxiety and lack of sleep can soon be alleviated. Having that extra bit of attention at home at night can make a big difference in your health and happiness. A night will be deemed awake if help is required more than twice throughout the course of the night.

Waking Night:
For a minimum of eight hours during the night, the carer is on duty. This is the ideal kind of assistance if you or a loved one repeatedly wakes up, possibly due to confusion or the need to use the loo. This is probably the best option if you have to give yourself medicine on a daily basis or if you wake up during the night frequently to adjust your position because of an injury.
For the remainder of the night, the waking night care provider is at your side, offering a high degree of professional support.

Companionship:
Life seems to slow down as we age. We at Rehoboth Healthcare Professionals are aware that arranging social events around a hectic schedule can be challenging at times. It might be relieving to know that there is a dependable, kind companion, whether it is for you or elderly relatives living locally or across the nation.
Among our most frequently requested services is companion care. When a Rehoboth Healthcare assistant offers companionship care, they develop into a reliable friend who can keep you or your loved one socially active in your own home and go with you or them to other social gatherings, family get-togethers, and friend visits.

Our companionship services at Rehoboth Healthcare Professionals may include a variety of activities for which we are able to offer assistance, such as:
- Generally speaking, friendship and dialogue
- Assistance with your weekly grocery shopping
- Participating in your preferred pastimes
- Assistance with routine household chores (laundry, cleaning, etc.)
- Assistance with making travel plans Taking you to your desired destination
- Making appointments and driving you there and back
- Organising trips and visits
- Our companionship services are designed to make sure that you and your loved ones can live contented, healthy, and fulfilling lives in the convenience of your own house.
